Topic

Our project idea is related to the global economy and the propose of this project is to educate the people by playing the game to simulates what will be happening in real-world economies. We currently have affairs in the economy, the things cause the issues are such as debt, natural disasters, disease, and more. Those lists of affairs are easy to cause to the economy in a bad way. Add to that even make small changes still has a big effect on the economy and people working. The statics produced by world bank (2020), the world GDP has grown for 4% in 2019, but at the same time, the debt has frown for 6%. As the evidence shown recently the debt is increasing more by year by year. Which represents the global economy getting worse every year.

Because the global economy's situation is getting worse, our project will be there to teach and let users understand by playing the game. To help the users to understand what will occur when they make a change. Also when we have some issues in society what will be caused to the economy. Our project it to simulates about the economy to help users to avoid making the same mistake in the real world and to what changes are needed to have a better global economy. It will help them to find out what is the best or right way to do to manage the economy without causing the issue and not increase the debt.
