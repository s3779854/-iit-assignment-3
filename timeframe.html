Timeframe

Our timeline is constrained through our ambitious project which requires extensive research and development. Within a 14 week timeline it is difficult to produce a final product, however our plan is detailed in the chart below:

In our initial 14 week scope, we aim to create a solid foundation for our game, in which features may be built around

The flow of our project attempts to map out attainable progress weekly. Initially, we create our ideas for the project and plan out how we will achieve our aims. Following/during this, we will hire skilled workers who will develop our program, whilst researching our environment, entry, global economics and the current virus (with its impact on the economy for future simulation)

During our design of our base game environment and its graphics, we will be working on our unity (c++) skills to be able to understand and develop our work further by ourselves, rather than relying on our hired workers.

During development and the whole process we aim to schedule frequent meetings to monitor our progress along side our plans. After some weeks of development we will begin alpha testing inhouse to detect any bugs, and produce our V0.1 product.
